# Account Management

The ForVoyez API provides a user-friendly dashboard for managing your account settings, subscriptions, billing, and payment history. In this section, we'll guide you through the various account management features available.

## User Account Settings

To access and modify your user account settings, follow these steps:

1. Sign in to your ForVoyez account and navigate to the [Dashboard](https://forvoyez.com/app).
2. Click on the "Account Settings" tab in the sidebar.
3. From the account settings page, you can:
- Update your profile information, such as your name and email address
- Change your password
- Configure notification preferences
- Manage API keys and tokens

Make sure to save any changes you make to your account settings.

## Subscription and Billing Management

ForVoyez offers various subscription plans to suit your API usage needs. To manage your subscription and billing details:

1. Sign in to your ForVoyez account and navigate to the [Dashboard](https://forvoyez.com/app).
2. Click on the "Billing" tab in the sidebar.
3. On the billing page, you can:
- View your current subscription plan and usage limits
- Upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan
- Update your billing information, such as your credit card details
- Cancel your subscription

Please note that changes to your subscription plan may take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Invoice and Payment History

ForVoyez provides access to your invoice and payment history for your records and reference. To view your invoice and payment history:

1. Sign in to your ForVoyez account and navigate to the [Dashboard](https://forvoyez.com/app).
2. Click on the "Billing" tab in the sidebar.
3. On the billing page, scroll down to the "Invoice History" section.
4. Here you can:
- View a list of your past invoices
- Download invoices as PDF files
- See the status of each invoice (paid, pending, or overdue)

If you have any questions or issues regarding your invoices or payments, please contact our [support team](https://forvoyez.com/contact) for assistance.
